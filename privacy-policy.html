We may collect the following categories of personal data:

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To improve the website and user experience

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases under GDPR Article 6:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): when you submit a contact form
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): to perform a contract
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): AML, KYC, regulatory compliance
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): website analytics and fraud prevention

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary or as required by law. Contact enquiry data is retained for up to 2 years. Client data is retained for at least 5 years following the end of the client relationship, as required by EU financial regulations.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right of access

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: ditrix.live@gmail.com
